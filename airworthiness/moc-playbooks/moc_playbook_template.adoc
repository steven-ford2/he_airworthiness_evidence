== 2. Compliance Philosophy by MoC Type

=== 2.1 Inspection

* Appropriate When:
** Physical attributes are directly observable
** Deterministic design criteria exist

* Inappropriate When:
** Performance margin must be empirically demonstrated
** Human variability is dominant

* Typical Evidence:
** Engineering drawings
** Configuration audit
** Requirements matrix
** Design compliance checklist

=== 2.2 Analysis

* Appropriate When:
** Analytical models exist (timeline analysis, workload modeling)
** Data simulation is feasible

* Inappropriate When:
** Empirical validation required by hazard severity

* Typical Evidence:
** Task timeline analysis
** Queuing model output
** Monte Carlo simulation
** Statistical model validation

=== 2.3 Demonstration

* Appropriate When:
** Controlled scenario replication possible
** SME evaluation required

* Inappropriate When:
** Hazard classification requires quantitative validation

* Typical Evidence:
** Demonstration video
** SME sign-off
** Scenario evaluation checklist

=== 2.4 Test

* Appropriate When:
** Criterion involves performance margin
** Environmental stressors relevant
** Human variability affects outcome

* Typical Evidence:
** Formal test report
** Raw data archive
** Statistical analysis report
** Traceable configuration record
