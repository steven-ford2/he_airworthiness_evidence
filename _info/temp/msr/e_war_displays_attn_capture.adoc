:artifact_type: MSR

== PURPOSE

Systematically review empirical evidence on how electronic warfare (EW) and threat-warning display characteristics (RWR/EW symbology, prioritization cues, alert modalities, nuisance rate, uncertainty presentation) influence pilot attention allocation, cognitive tunneling, decision accuracy, and mission performance in tactical or high-tempo aviation contexts.

This review focuses on: attentional capture, alert storms, nuisance/false alarm effects, and trust/reliance behaviors.
